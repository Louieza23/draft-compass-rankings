What is Best Ball?
Best Ball formats are draft-only fantasy football leagues where you don't need to worry about setting lineups or making waiver decisions. Each week, your best scoring lineup is automatically selected after the games have been played. Weekly points are added up and the team with the most points at the end of the season wins.
